Mathematics -- Number Theory

Description

Pick any natural number (1, 2, 3, ...). If it's even, divide it by 2.
If it's odd, multiply it by 3 and add 1. Keep doing this, and stop if you get to 1 (for example, 10 --> 5 --> 16 --> 8 --> 4 --> 2 --> 1). Question: do you always get to 1, no matter what number you pick?
